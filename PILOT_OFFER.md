# AI Workflow Assurance Sprint

**Demand-test price:** £7,500 + VAT
**Duration:** 15 business days
**Scope:** Three sanitised AI-built project workflows

The price remains a hypothesis until buyers pay it.

## Included

- one intended-use, owner, data and reliance record per workflow;
- browser/static inspection plus approved specialist reports;
- one evidence-linked assurance report per workflow;
- no more than three fix-first actions per workflow;
- one joint readout with builders and the client's IT, Data or Risk reviewer;
- remediation guidance and one re-check for one selected workflow;
- one human decision record per workflow;
- a trust and rebuy interview.

## Client responsibilities

- name a sponsor and each workflow owner;
- select three real workflows;
- provide synthetic, sanitised or approved client-controlled artefacts;
- provide an authorised IT, Data, Security, Risk or Legal reviewer;
- make every reliance and production decision;
- approve retention, deletion and any model or specialist-tool route.

## Excluded

- certification, penetration testing or legal advice;
- broad shadow-AI discovery;
- production hosting;
- automatic remediation;
- processing uncontrolled live sensitive data;
- production approval by Newco or its software.

## Payment hypothesis

- 50% on signed order;
- 50% after the decision readout.

## Success evidence

The pilot counts only when the evidence ledger contains:

- signed scope and payment evidence;
- approved artefact manifests;
- three initial reports;
- one before-and-after re-check;
- named decisions and conditions;
- buyer answers to trust and rebuy questions;
- delivery hours and direct costs;
- agreed deletion or retention evidence.
