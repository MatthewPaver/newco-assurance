## Blind report reviews

Give three reviewers the exported report without a product walkthrough.

Record:

- the decision they think the evidence supports;
- the first action they would take;
- any claim they reject;
- the evidence their existing process still requires;
- whether the builder could act without a developer translating the report.

## Decision gate

Continue productisation only when:

- at least one non-dependent client pays for the sprint;
- the evidence changes or narrows a real reliance decision;
- a builder completes a priority remediation;
- an IT/Data reviewer says the report is trustworthy enough to act on;
- delivery cost supports a viable margin;
- the buyer gives a clear rebuy answer.

Stop or reshape when buyers see only a duplicate scanner report, cannot act on the findings or will not pay before estate-wide features exist.
